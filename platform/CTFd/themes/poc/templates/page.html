Rules and how it works

1st rule of PoCTF is : you do not complain about the challenges.
2nd rule of PoCTF is : you do not complain about the challenges.
3rd rule : You do not attack the platform itself or any services attached to poc-innovation.com (Big Brother is watching you).
4th rule : You do not attack other participants. This is a CTF, not a street fight.

To solve a challenge, there is only one objective : find the flag and submit it.
Flags are not shared (obviously).
